Seongbo Jang is currently a visiting scholar in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Yonsei University. He is expected to receive his Ph.D. in Computer Science from POSTECH, under the supervision of Prof. Hwanjo Yu. During his doctoral studies, he worked as a machine learning researcher at Scatter Lab, where he contributed to the development of Luda Lee, a social companion chatbot, and Zeta, an AI storytelling platform, both of which serve over one million users. His research focuses on building engaging and empathetic conversational agents, with the goal of exploring desirable human-like behaviors to develop more socially intelligent agents.
